# Wireless ADB Setup for Samsung Fold 7

## Enable Wireless Debugging on Your Fold 7

### Step 1: Enable Developer Options (if not already done)
1. Go to **Settings** → **About phone** → **Software information**
2. Tap **Build number** 7 times
3. Enter your PIN/password when prompted

### Step 2: Enable Wireless Debugging
1. Go to **Settings** → **Developer options**
2. Scroll down to **Wireless debugging**
3. Turn ON the **Wireless debugging** toggle
4. Tap on **Wireless debugging** to enter the menu

### Step 3: Pair Your Device
1. In Wireless debugging menu, tap **Pair device with pairing code**
2. Note the pairing code, IP address, and port number shown
3. Keep this screen open
